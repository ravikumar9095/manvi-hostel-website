Nearby Essentials

Jaya Hospital

A trusted hospital located just 140 meters from the hostel. Offers emergency care, general medicine, and diagnostics. Open 24/7.

MedPlus Pharmacy

A well-stocked pharmacy with both generic and branded medicines. Only a 3-minute walk from the PG. Also provides basic health kits and sanitary items.

MORE SUPERMARKET

Convenient general store offering groceries, stationery, snacks, and household items. Just 140 meters away from the hostel entrance.

Vanasthalipuram Bus Stop

Major public transport hub with frequent buses connecting to Dilsukhnagar, LB Nagar, and other key areas of Hyderabad. Only 300 meters from the PG.
